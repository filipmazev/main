INFOMOBILITY

Fluid mobility also depends on the availability of high quality information to infrastructure operators and road users. This requires wide-area traffic and mobility monitoring, real-time traffic modelling, forecasting and strategic management & Multi-channel traffic information services. All of these features are brought together by MISTIC: the integrated platform offers a rapidly growing portfolio of services for traffic managers, businesses and individual travellers. MISTIC supports Traffic Control Centres by monitoring mobility on the road network in real time MISTIC engines fuse data from many sources to build up a detailed picture of traffic behaviour and make forecasts. MISTIC uses multiple channels to provide road users with quality information services.
