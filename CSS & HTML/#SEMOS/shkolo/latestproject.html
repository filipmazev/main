LATEST PROJECTS

EVN MACEDONIA AD SKOPJE : Involvement in major recent regional projects, e.g.: For the needs of EVN Macedonia, AD Skopje, our company was working on telemetry system for supervision, installing remote reading system which is collecting data from collectors and other devices located on measuring stations, then storing and transmitting data to a central location and processing collected data. This project total amount was EUR 450.000. SKOPJE URBAN TRANSPORT PROJECT AUTOMATED TRAFFIC: Since January 2013, we are working on this project like general subcontractor of our partner SWARCO AG . •This project improved traffic in the city of Skopje as: significantly is reduced travelling time, waiting for the passage of intersections, fuel consumption, pollution from traffic, etc. Contract for this project is signed until October 31, 2018. This project "Skopje Urban Transport Project – Automated Traffic Management" and its total amount is EUR 2.500.000 excluding VAT. Our scope of works was complete implementation of this project, starting from making of project documentation, construction works in TMCC, all field works, installation of all equipment in TMCC, all type of cabling and power supply, making 10GB network installation in TMCC and on the field, making fiberoptic network connecting all intersections with TMCC, installing and connecting of the 57 intersection cabinets, programing of controllers and other equipment in cabinets, installing all hardware and software InTMCC and server room, making 1170 inductive loops, installing of 56 cameras on the crosings , etc . •Full maintenance and service on the all crossings and TMCC for 5 years.
